How It Works
Round 1 — Random draw. All 4 courts run simultaneously.
Rounds 2–4 — Swiss Ladder: winners play winners, losers play losers. Every matchup gets more competitive each round.
No eliminations — every team plays all 4 games. Final placement by win-loss record. Tiebreaker: head-to-head → point differential.
Player Management
Organizer Tool: Tap a player's button to mark them absent. Once you've marked all no-shows, hit Rejigger Teams to redistribute available players into balanced teams and refresh the full schedule.
